How It's Made

1. Material Selection

We take pride in making our products durable and safe, that's why we're using stainless steel (food-grade 18/8 steel)

2. Raw Material Preparation

Recycled metal is often used to reduce environmental impact.
We clean our steel and cut it into perfect sheets.

3. Giving Shape

The cut sheets are pressed, stretched, or molded into cylindrical shapes.
Bottom and body are joined seamlessly to ensure leak resistance.

4. Reinforcing Durability

Bottles are heat-treated to harden the metal.
This improves durability and resistance to dents.

5. Unique, modern style

Inside: Food-safe, BPA-free lining prevents metallic taste.
Outside: Powder coating or eco-friendly paint adds color, texture, and grip.

6. Quality Assurance

Every bottle is tested for leaks, safety, and durability before packaging in recyclable materials.
